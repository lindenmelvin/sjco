San José Youth Orchestras

The mission of the San José Youth Chamber Orchestras is to equip young string students with the abilities needed for excellent performance, as well as to inspire a love for music and the development of life skills such as teamwork and discipline. The four youth chamber orchestras are the recipients of coaching and mentoring by the professional artists of the Chamber Orchestra, as well as guidance by their wonderful conductors.

Weekly rehearsals culminate in several concerts per year, as well as invitations to special events, such as performances for Mentor Graphics and Cathedral Basilica of St. Joseph’s Season of Hope. Masterclasses, field trips and the occasional senior orchestra tour round out the year's activities.

Each season is concludes with a Side-by-Side Concert, with students of the Youth Orchestra performing alongside members of the San José Chamber Orchestra.

San José Senior Youth Chamber Orchestra

Led by Tony Quartuccio, the Senior Youth Chamber Orchestra performs seminal works in the string orchestra repertoire. The orchestra performs several concerts a year and periodically at special events and a tour. Students participate in three to four concerts per year, and are instructed in advanced chamber orchestra technique. The season culminates in a side-by-side performance with members of the San José Chamber Orchestra.

San José Intermediate Youth Chamber Orchestra

Under the direction of Susan Stein, the Intermediate Youth Orchestra performs works that will provide the students with a deeper understanding of ensemble playing. Through playing, students will be provided the tools that will prepare them for the standard string orchestra repertoire. Students will develop more advanced techniques, with music taken directly from the String Chamber repertoire. This group performs three concerts during the season.

San José Preparatory Youth Chamber Orchestra

Led by Susan Stein, the Preparatory Youth Chamber Orchestra gives students a strengthening of the fundamentals and is designed to be the first orchestral experience for young music students. Students focus on ensemble techniques as they explore chamber orchestra repertoire. This group presents three concerts during the season.

Santa Cruz Chamber Strings-Intermediate

Under the baton of Cynthia Baehr, Santa Cruz Chamber Strings-Intermediate provides students with the skills needed for excellent performances. Youth Orchestra members are the recipients of coaching and mentoring by professional musicians.
